Beginner Workout Plans

Bench Press

Targets chest, shoulders, and triceps muscles

Reps: 8-10

Rest: 90 seconds

Target: Chest, Shoulders, Triceps

Dumbbell Row

Essential for developing back muscles and strength

Sets: 3

Reps: 10-12

Rest: 60 seconds

Target: Back, Biceps

Squats

The king of lower body exercises for mass and strength

Sets: 3

Reps: 10-12

Rest: 90 seconds

Target: Quadriceps, Hamstrings, Glutes

Treadmill Intervals

Efficient fat burning through high intensity intervals

Sets: 5-8 intervals

Work: 30 seconds (high intensity)

Rest: 90 seconds (low intensity)

Target: Cardiovascular System, Full Body

Circuit Training

Full body workout keeps heart rate elevated for maximum calorie burn

Sets: 3 circuits

Exercises: 6-8 different movements

Rest: Minimal between exercises

Target: Full Body, Metabolic Conditioning

Dynamic movements that engage the whole body while burning calories

Sets: 3

Reps: 15-20

Rest: 45 seconds

Target: Core, Shoulders, Explosive Power
